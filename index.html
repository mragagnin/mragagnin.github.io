About

Strategy sharpened by experience. Delivered with a human touch.

I've spent my career at the intersection of strategy, technology, and people. From co-founding startups in France to leading multi-million dollar transformation programmes at AWS across Asia-Pacific, I've learned that the best outcomes happen when sharp thinking meets genuine collaboration.

Originally from France, I've called Sydney home for over a decade — bringing a global perspective with deep roots in the ANZ market. I don't parachute in with a pre-packaged answer and disappear. I take the time to understand your specific context, work alongside your teams, and stay committed until the work delivers lasting value.

When I'm not working, you'll find me training for a marathon or triathlon. That same discipline of showing up, going the distance, and pushing through obstacles and uncertainty is something I bring to every engagement.
